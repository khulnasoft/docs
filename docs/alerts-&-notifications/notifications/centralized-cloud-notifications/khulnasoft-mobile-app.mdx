## Setup

### Prerequisites

- A Khulnasoft Cloud account
- You need to have the Khulnasoft Mobile App installed on your [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cloud.khulnasoft.android&pli=1) or [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/khulnasoft-mobile/id6474659622) phone.

### Khulnasoft Mobile App Configuration and device linking

In order to login to the Khulnasoft Mobile App

1. Download the Khulnasoft Mobile App from [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cloud.khulnasoft.android&pli=1) or the [iOS App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/khulnasoft-mobile/id6474659622)
2. Open the App and Choose your Sign-in option
  - Email Address: Enter the email address of your registered Khulnasoft Cloud account and click on the verification link received by email on your mobile device.
  - Sign-in with QR Code: Scan the QR code from the Khulnasoft Cloud UI under **Profile Picture** --> **Settings** --> **Notifications** --> **Mobile App Notifications** --> **Show QR Code**

### Khulnasoft Configuration

After linking your device, enable the toggle for **Mobile App Notifications** under the same settings panel.
